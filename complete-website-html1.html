Technical Specifications

The Pangea STORM combines cutting-edge aerodynamics with robust engineering to deliver unprecedented cargo transportation efficiency.

  • Dimensions: 12m wingspan
  • Max Payload: 600 kg
  • Range: 500 km (full payload), 800 km (reduced payload)
  • Power System: Hybrid electric-combustion with 200 kW output
  • Cruise Speed: 200 km/h
  • Operating Conditions: -20°C to +45°C, wind up to 25 knots
  • Security: End-to-end encryption, redundant communication systems
