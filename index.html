Our Journey

The evolution of Pangea from concept to revolutionary cargo transportation solution

2020

The Beginning

Pangea founded by Dr. Sarah Chen and Michael Rodriguez after collaborative research at MIT's Aeronautics Lab on ground effect vehicles for commercial applications.

2021

First Prototype

Successful development and test flight of our first small-scale prototype, demonstrating the viability of our ground effect technology for cargo applications.

2022

Series A Funding

Secured $18 million in Series A funding led by Blue Horizon Ventures to scale our technology and build a full-size demonstration model.

2023

GE-100 Launch

Unveiled our first production model, the GE-100, capable of carrying 500kg payload over 300km. Began pilot programs with select logistics partners.

2024

Regulatory Approval

Obtained FAA certification for commercial operations in designated corridors. Established partnerships with major coastal shipping companies.

2025

GE-200 Revolution

Launching our flagship GE-200 model with 2-ton capacity and advanced autonomous features, revolutionizing mid-range cargo transport with 40% cost reduction compared to traditional methods.
