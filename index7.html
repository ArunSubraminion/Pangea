Technical Specifications

The Pangea GE-200 combines cutting-edge aerodynamics with robust engineering to deliver unprecedented cargo transportation efficiency.

  • Dimensions: 15m length × 12m wingspan × 3.5m height
  • Max Payload: 2,000 kg
  • Range: 500 km (full payload), 800 km (reduced payload)
  • Power System: Hybrid electric-combustion with 180 kW output
  • Cruise Speed: 200 km/h
  • Operating Conditions: -20°C to +45°C, wind up to 25 knots
  • Security: End-to-end encryption, redundant communication systems

Our Journey

The evolution of Pangea from concept to revolutionary cargo transportation solution

2025

The Beginning

First prototype developed - BREEZE: 1.5m scale aircraft serving as a 5kg technology demonstrator to validate our ground effect system design and engineering principles.

2026

GALE Development

4.5m scale demonstrator which can also be serial produced for specific use cases. GALE proves commercial viability with field tests in various conditions.

2027

STORM Prototype

12m full scale prototype cargo Ground Effect Aircraft capable of carrying significant payloads across medium-range routes with unmatched efficiency.

2028

STORM Production

Serial production begins for STORM models, with multiple units deployed to logistics partners for commercial operations in coastal and over-water routes.

2029

HURRICANE Design

Detailed design phase for HURRICANE, our 40m class aircraft for efficient large-scale cargo transportation, capable of revolutionizing medium and long-range shipping.
